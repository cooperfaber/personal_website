Project 5: Exploring High-Level Graphics Libraries.

This program has a Shiba Inu model dance for the amusement of the viewer, with animations linked to the BPM of the song. The animation, once it begins, will lock the camera, as the camera moves during the animation. Note that the animation is optimized specifcally for Tunak Tunak Tun.
Please wait at least 2 seconds when first starting before beginning animations, as models need to load.
System Requirements: The HTML audio tag is used, which requires an up-to-date browser. You also need an active internet connection.
Please verify that your browser is above this version if you have trouble:
Chrome: 4.0
Firefox: 3.5
Microsoft Edge: 9.0
Apple Safari: 4.0

Available Songs:
Tunak Tunak Tun - Daler Mehndi
Let's Dance - David Bowie
I've Seen Footage - Death Grips

Camera controls are:
W&S: Move camera forward and back
Click&Hold: Camera pans towards mouse movement

To activate animation:
T: Dance to Daler Mehndi
B: Dance to David Bowie
N->O->I->D (in sequence): Dance to Death Grips
